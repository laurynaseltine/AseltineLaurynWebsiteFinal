WELCOME TO OUR LIL FAMILY

Welcome to Kasama, where Filipino flavors meet Chicago flair! As a vibrant restaurant nestled in the heart of the Windy City, we pride ourselves on crafting authentic Filipino dishes with a modern twist. At Kasama, our name embodies our philosophy: "Kasama" means "together" in Filipino. Just like our diverse community, our dishes celebrate the beauty of unity and togetherness. Our logo symbolizes this sentiment, with its elegant design standing strong on its own while also reflecting the idea that things are always better when experienced together. Join us at Kasama for a culinary journey that celebrates the richness of Filipino cuisine and the joy of sharing delicious meals with loved ones.
